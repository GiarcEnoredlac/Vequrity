Welcome to Vequrity - Train your cybersecurity team, the most effective way.

A cloud-based platform for developing, selling and executing live, hands-on cyber trainings on real systems.

Vequrity helps IT administrators to learn cyber defense by training with threats in a safe, cloud based IT environment. Every Day.

We've developed LiveX. A platform designed to support game based, practical and continuous training for the entire team of IT aministrators. Trainees can rehearse defense tactics on real IT services in a safe environment. LiveX can simulate a range of attacks and provide immediate feedback.
